Why Worship With Us?

  • 1) A Prepared Pastor:

    Conderidge Smith is a life-long preacher, called by god to preach at the age of 12. graduated from New York University (B.A,), Yale University (M.Div.), married with children, and anointed to pastor with visionary leadership and God's love.

  • 2) A Purposeful People:

    Agape is full of loving people serving a loving God seeking to a God-sized vision, which includes multiple locations for worship, a bible school, pre-K through college, recreation center, community service center, and evangelism to the Detroit Metro area.

  • 3) A Precise Point:

    What is our point? To receive and express the love of God in every area of life as individuals, families, and as a church.
